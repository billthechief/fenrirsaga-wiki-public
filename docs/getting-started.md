To connect to the server, please follow these steps in order:

1.  **Download:** Get the latest Fenrir Saga client from our [official website](https://fenrirsaga.com/#/download).
2.  **Extract:** Unzip the downloaded file. We recommend placing the folder in your `C:\Users\` directory for best performance.
3.  **Configure:** Run `Setup.exe` to configure your preferred screen size and graphics settings.
4.  **Register:** Create your Master Account on the website's control panel. This account will manage all of your in-game accounts.
5.  **Create Game Account:** Once your Master Account is set up, create a new Game Account from the control panel. This is what you'll use to log in to the game.
6.  **Launch:** Double-click `Fenrir Saga Patcher.exe` to update the client and start the game.

#### ğŸ’¬ **In-Game Channels**
In-game chat channels are server-wide channels that you can use to communicate with other players. When you first log in, you will automatically join the `#main`, `#trade`, and `#support` channels.

**How to use channels:**
To speak in a specific channel, type the channel name (e.g., `#main`) into the recipient box on the left of your chat window, then type your message and press Enter.

* `#main`: The general channel for all-purpose chat with the community.
* `#trade`: Used for buying, selling, and price-checking items.
* `#support`: Ask questions about the game, quests, or mechanics.
* `#party`: Use this channel when you are **L**ooking **F**or a **G**roup or party members.
* `#map`: For chat restricted to players on your current map.
* `#ally`: A private channel for communication between your guild and any allied guilds.

!!! tip "Discover More Channels"
    You can see a complete list of available public channels and other useful at-commands by typing `@channel list` directly into your chat window.
